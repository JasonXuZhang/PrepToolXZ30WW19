County Commissioners

County commissioners are in charge of the county government. There are usually at least three individuals on the county commission, but there can be more.

County commissioners approve the county’s budget and also set the county tax rate. They are in charge of making sure the county roads are well maintained. They also have final approval of all sub-division planning and waste water treatment. In some counties, county commissioners have responsibility for jails, fire departments, libraries, parks, and schools.


How to vote

Vote for up to 5.

To vote, touch a name. A check mark ( ) will appear to show your choice.

If you want to change your vote or if you make a mistake, first touch the blue box you no longer want. That box will turn white. Then, touch the choice you do want.

To not vote in this contest, just touch Skip.

  • To erase, touch Erase.

To finish, touch Accept. If you change your mind, touch Cancel.
